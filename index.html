About me

My name is Mitchel Kappen. I grew up in the Netherlands as the youngest of 5 kids. I am creative and I like solving problems, always have.

During high school I attended a bilingual program, because I was always intrigued by the English language. After that, I went to do my bachelor's in Cognitive and Neurobiological Psychology at Utrecht University.

After that I went on to do my master's in Applied Cognitive Psychology and my PhD in Health Sciences at Ghent University, in Belgium.

In the meanwhile, I've worked and studied at the University of Victoria in Canada and at Stanford University in the United States. But enough about that, you can find that over here!


> I like to do many different sports, such as squash, padel, and volleyball, and always in for a board game or two.

During my education and jobs, I have developed myself to be quick on my feet. I can talk in debt on statistics and methodology, but have a strong affinity with business development.

By the way, check out my awesome work.
